About Me

✻

My name is Anastasia, and I am a part-time photographer. My main occupation is medical assistant, and I’m currently a medical student pursuing a sonographer degree. Photography has been my hobby for the last 5 years. I believe in capturing happiness. Our lives are so hectic, we are always busy, trying to achieve our goals, trying to build a better future. When looking back we often find ourselves missing the most important thing - time spent with our loved ones. So let’s get together and create memories in photographs, let’s capture happiness together.
